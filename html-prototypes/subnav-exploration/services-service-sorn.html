Service performance SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification)

Service to make a SORN. This must be made for a registered vehicle that is not being used on the road, and which has been taxed since 31 January 1998.

Users on start page

  • Data source: Google Analytics

    Shows the estimated number of users currently accessing the GOV.UK start page for the SORN transaction.

    Users on this page have not started the transaction at this point.

Customer satisfaction

  • Data source: Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency database

    Customer satisfaction with the SORN/Tax disc transaction is recorded on a scale of 1 to 5. 1 being most satisfied and 5 being least satisfied. The mean of these responses is converted to a percentage to display on the dashboard.

Average score of satisfied responses
